01 — About

hello there!

Orlando, FL · UCF Graduate · 3+ Years

I am an impassioned and driven designer based out of Orlando, Florida. Using my expertise in both graphic and web design, I have been developing unique web and mobile experiences for students for over four years.

My skillset ensures that the digital experiences I develop are intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing to all. Though I am an artist and storyteller by trade, I am an educator at heart. I leverage my experience as a researcher to merge education and interactivity for students of all ages. My mission is to ensure that quality and dynamic educational content is available for everyone.

When I'm not developing or researching, I enjoy drawing, watching classic movies, finding new things to learn about, and giving back to my community!
